What safe drug & alcohol strategies can you suggest to enhance the safety and wellbeing of yourself and others?

Safe drug and alcohol strategies to enhance the safety and well-being of yourself and others are few and far between, but there are some of note. The most obvious is drinking in controlled amounts and such that you are not too intoxicated, but another is to be with friends and people you trust when you do choose to drink. Being at a club or bar by yourself with no one you know is much less safe than being with people you know and trust. This is because they can help you when you are under the influence and assist you with certain tasks such as driving. Therefore, some strategies to maximise the health and safety of yourself and others in drug and alcohol-related situations are drinking in controlled portions if you do drink and having somebody you know and trust with you.
